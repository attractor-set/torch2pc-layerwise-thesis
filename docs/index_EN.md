# Research documentation

[Русская версия](index.md)

The documentation connects the research question, protocol, [execution](glossary_EN.md#term-execution),
[evidence](glossary_EN.md#term-evidence), and bounded findings. Plans remain separate from observations, and
published results always include their scope and [artifact provenance](glossary_EN.md#term-provenance).

## Current state

- Stage 1 and Stage 2 are complete immutable published baselines;
- Stage 3A diagnostics and statistical publication are complete;
- Stage 3B B0 canonical [execution](glossary_EN.md#term-execution), validation, [integrity sealing](glossary_EN.md#term-integrity-sealing), and [evidence](glossary_EN.md#term-evidence)
  publication are complete;
- Stage 3B B0 statistical and engineering analysis is complete and published;
- the full Stage 3B program remains incomplete;
- the next stage is [candidate](glossary_EN.md#term-candidate)-specific numerical-equivalence testing for B1
  and B2.

The machine-readable boundary remains unchanged:
`full_stage3b_campaign_complete=false`.

## Reading order

1. [Current status](https://github.com/attractor-set/torch2pc-layerwise-thesis/blob/main/STATUS_EN.md)
   contains only verified state and bounded findings.
2. [Roadmap](https://github.com/attractor-set/torch2pc-layerwise-thesis/blob/main/ROADMAP_EN.md)
   defines the remaining sequence and transition criteria.
3. Protocols and preregistration define rules before [execution](glossary_EN.md#term-execution).
4. Result directories and reports record observations after [execution](glossary_EN.md#term-execution).
5. The [research glossary](glossary_EN.md) fixes term meanings and
   Russian–English equivalence.
6. The [language and terminology policy](https://github.com/attractor-set/torch2pc-layerwise-thesis/blob/main/LANGUAGE_POLICY_EN.md)
   defines how terms are selected, added, and changed.
