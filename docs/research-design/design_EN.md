# Research design

[Русская версия](design.md)

The study observes BP, Exact, FixedPred, and Strict within one shared
computational framework. Directional conclusions are deferred until the
experiment is completed.

The primary axes are [dataset](../glossary_EN.md#term-dataset), [architecture](../glossary_EN.md#term-architecture), method, state-inference budget,
optimizer, [model seed](../glossary_EN.md#term-model-seed), and compute budget. The primary scope is limited to
FashionMNIST/MNIST and `lenet_classic`; extensions are marked as secondary.
